Lasagna

Odin's Favorite Lasagna

This lasagna was created for the All-Father himself, after numerous trials and tribulations within the realm of Musphelheim. Now, you too can create the legendary dish!

Ingredients Needed

  • Meat of a Fire Beast
  • Volcanic Onions
  • Lava Tomatoes
  • Ashen Sugar
  • Brimstone Spices and Seasonings
  • Cheese from a Smoke Goat
  • Eggs

How to make it, Step-By-Step but not really

  1. Make the meat sauce through the heat of a small star
  2. Cook your noodles within the depths of Surtr's Trials
  3. Concoct a Ricotta mixture that would melt mortal men
  4. Add cheese
  5. Layer the ingredients in order of the Gauntlet of Fire
  6. Cover in magical smoke and let cook
  7. Survive the soul melting flavor

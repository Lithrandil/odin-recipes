Buffalo Wings

Midgard's Famous Wings

These wings are known throughout all the nine-realms as a go-to dinner! Simple and easy to follow, this meal is a treat for any and all.

Ingredients Needed

  • Chicken Wings -- Drums or Flats
  • Buffalo Sauce
  • Garlic, Salt, Pepper
  • Optional: Flour

How to make them, Step-By-Step

  1. Once Thawed, pat completely dry all wings
  2. In a mixing bowl, toss dried wings in Seasonings to ensure a good coating
  3. Cook within an oven for 40 minutes, turning wings over halfway through
  4. Let standing cook for an additional 5 minutes
  5. Coat generously in Buffalo Sauce

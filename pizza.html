Vanaheim's Classic Cheese Pizza

Made with earthy herbs and delicious cheese, this pizza gives the flavors of the Vanaheim forests a tribute of love.

Ingredients Needed

  • Kneaded Dough
  • Yak Cheese
  • Mix of local Vanaheim spices
  • Enlarged Tomatoes for Sauce

How to make it, Step-By-Step

  1. Knead dough into circular shape, with thick or thin consistency
  2. Crush and layer tomatoes for sauce
  3. Add first layer of Vanaheim spices
  4. Add cheese
  5. Top with more spices to flavor
  6. Put within the open flame and let cook till crust is firm and cheese golden brown at parts
  7. Enjoy!
